About Me

Welcome! I am Arnab Chakraborty, a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Southern California, specializing in computational chemistry and quantum mechanics. So far, my research focuses in developing advanced methodologies in electronic structure theory, such as coupled-cluster, equation-of-motion coupled cluster, and density functional theories, to address challenging problems in molecular sciences.

Currently, I am working on implementing density based theories in QChem. In addition, I am also exploring computational drug discovery techniques and machine learning applications to chemical and biological problems.

Let's Connect

When I am not immersed in research or coding, I enjoy exploring advancements in machine learning, designing creative graphics with various open-source software, and looking up open-source projects. Feel free to reach out to collaborate or discuss exciting projects in computational chemistry and beyond!
